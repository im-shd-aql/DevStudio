Questions & Answers

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Sprouts and plant care.

Is Sprouts free to use?

+
Yes! Sprouts offers a free version with core features including watering reminders, basic plant care guides, and access to our community. We also offer Sprouts Premium with advanced features like detailed analytics, unlimited plant profiles, AI plant identification, and ad-free experience for $4.99/month or $39.99/year.

How does the smart watering system work?

+
Our AI-powered system considers multiple factors including plant species, pot size, soil type, local weather conditions, season, and your home environment. It learns from your input and adjusts reminders to ensure optimal watering schedules. You'll receive notifications at the perfect time, preventing both overwatering and underwatering.

Can I identify plants using the app?

+
Absolutely! With Sprouts Premium, you can use our AI-powered plant identification feature. Simply take a photo of any plant, and our advanced recognition system will identify it from our database of over 10,000 species. You'll instantly receive detailed care instructions and can add it to your collection with one tap.

What if I travel and can't water my plants?

+
Sprouts includes a "Travel Mode" feature! Before you leave, activate travel mode and specify your travel dates. The app will provide pre-travel care instructions, suggest which plants need extra attention, and can even recommend self-watering solutions. You can also share temporary access with friends or family who'll help care for your plants while you're away.

Does Sprouts work offline?

+
Yes! Once you've added your plants and downloaded their care guides, you can access basic features offline including viewing your plant collection, care schedules, and saved guides. Reminders will still function offline. However, features like plant identification, community access, and weather-based updates require an internet connection.

How do I cancel my Premium subscription?

+
You can cancel anytime directly from your device's subscription settings (App Store or Google Play). Your Premium features will remain active until the end of your billing period, and you won't be charged again. Even after canceling, you'll keep all your plant data and can continue using the free version of Sprouts.

Is my plant data backed up?

+
Yes! All your plant data, photos, notes, and care history are automatically backed up to the cloud. If you switch devices or reinstall the app, simply log in with your account and everything will be restored. We use encrypted storage to ensure your data is secure and private.
